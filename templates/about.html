Project Overview

Treasure Island: Carbon Quest is a thrilling game where players travel between airports, collecting gold while managing their CO2 budget. Explore diverse destinations, solve puzzles, and strategize to extend your adventure. Can you gather riches before your carbon footprint hits its limit?

The user needs to click on an airport from the map to travel to it and then on the button that says 'collect' to collect the gold to reach the target.
